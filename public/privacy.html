01Who We Are

This website and the Trading Journal application ("Service") are operated by Parth Ranjan, an individual. The Service is a personal trading journal platform available at pr-website-omega.vercel.app (and any associated domain).

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at parthranjan2025@gmail.com.

02Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to provide the Service:

  • Account data — email address and encrypted password (handled entirely by Supabase Auth)
  • Profile data — display name you optionally provide during signup
  • Trade data — trade entries you log: instrument, date, direction, size, entry/exit prices, result, notes, screenshots
  • Usage data — basic analytics (page views, errors) collected by Vercel's edge infrastructure — no personally identifying information

We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

03How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To display and store your trade journal entries
  • To compute performance analytics (win rate, P&L, drawdown) shown in your dashboard
  • To show anonymised aggregate public stats on the Journal landing page (total trades count only — no individual data)
  • To respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

04Data Sharing & Third Parties

We use a minimal set of sub-processors to operate the Service:

Sub-processors

Supabase (supabase.com) — Database, authentication, and file storage. Data is hosted on AWS infrastructure. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. Supabase Privacy Policy →

Vercel (vercel.com) — Hosting and CDN. Collects minimal edge logs (IP, user-agent). Vercel is GDPR-compliant. Vercel Privacy Policy →

Google Fonts — Font delivery CDN. Minimal request metadata. Google Privacy Policy →

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties. We do not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, or social media SDKs.

05Storage & Security

All data is stored in Supabase's cloud database hosted on AWS (us-east-1 region). Access is protected by:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) — your trade data is only accessible by your authenticated session
  • Encrypted passwords — Supabase Auth never stores plaintext passwords (bcrypt)
  • HTTPS everywhere — all data in transit is TLS-encrypted
  • JWT-based sessions — short-lived access tokens with refresh rotation

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure. Do not store highly sensitive information (e.g., broker API keys, banking credentials) in trade notes.

06Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — Export your complete trade history from the Journal → Settings tab at any time
  • Correction — Edit any trade entry directly in the Journal app
  • Deletion — Delete individual trades or request full account deletion by emailing us
  • Portability — Export your data as CSV/JSON from the Settings tab
  • Object — Contact us to opt out of any specific processing

To request account deletion or a full data export, email parthranjan2025@gmail.com with subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 7 days.

07Cookies & Local Storage

We use no third-party tracking cookies. The Service uses browser storage only for:

  • pr-theme — your dark/light theme preference (localStorage)
  • sb-* — Supabase authentication session tokens (localStorage) — required for login to work

There are no marketing cookies, analytics cookies, or cross-site trackers. Clearing your browser's local storage will log you out.

08Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, if the changes are significant, notify registered users by email.

Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
