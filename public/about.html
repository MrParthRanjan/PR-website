About Me

Trader · Strategist · Systems Thinker

I'm Parth — a full-time intraday trader with 4+ years in forex, gold, and crypto markets. My edge isn't in complex algorithms or AI predictions. It's in understanding price action, respecting risk, and executing with precision.

Everything I trade, I build systems around. Every loss, I analyze ruthlessly. Every win, I reverse-engineer.

Core Philosophy

🎯 Price Action First

Charts don't lie. News, sentiment, macro — they all move price. I read what the market is actually doing, not what I think it should do.

⏰ Session Precision

Not all hours are equal. London open, NY open — these have personality. I trade when conditions match my edge.

🛡️ Risk First, Profit Second

Position sizing isn't boring. It's the only thing that actually compounds wealth. Every trade starts with: "How much am I willing to lose?"

⚙️ Systems > Feelings

Emotions are expensive. I build rules, I follow them, I backtest them. The moment I improvise, I lose money.

The Journey

2020 — Starting Out

Learned forex on demo, blew up real account in 3 weeks. Realized I knew nothing. Started reading, studying, failing.

2021 — Finding the Edge

Stopped chasing indicators. Went back to basics — support, resistance, structure. Started paper trading seriously. First consistent month.

2022 — Building Systems

Started tracking trades obsessively. Built my own Excel journal. Realized psychology was 80% of the game. Focused on execution, not prediction.

2023–2024 — Going Full-Time

Scaled account from $5K to consistent monthly income. Added gold and crypto. Built frameworks for different market conditions. Now trade live daily.

2025 — Sharing the Craft

Building this site. Not to teach, but to document. Not to sell a course, but to show what real trading looks like — wins, losses, the whole picture.
