ABOUT

Parth Ranjan

Trader · Strategist · Systems Thinker

I'm Parth — a full-time intraday trader with 4+ years in forex, gold, and crypto markets. My edge isn't in complex algorithms or AI predictions. It's in understanding price action, respecting risk, and executing with precision.

Everything I trade, I build systems around. Every loss, I analyze ruthlessly. Every win, I reverse-engineer.

My Journey

2020 — Discovery
Started trading during the pandemic. Learned that passion isn't enough — systems are.
2021 — Struggle & Learning
Lost significant capital. Realized most traders fail because they don't follow rules. Built my first systematic approach.
2022 — Consistency Emerges
Price action mastery. Session-aware strategies. Profitable across Forex, Gold, and Crypto.
2023 — Scaling & Teaching
Moved from survival to strategy optimization. Started documenting trades and sharing insights.
2024 — Platform Building
Live journal, systematic analysis, and a focus on replicability. Not rocket science — just discipline.

Trading Philosophy

Price Action First

No lagging indicators. The market is the market. Structure, liquidity, and psychology — that's all you need.

Session Precision

London & NY opens. Not random entries. Calculated, session-aware execution during high-probability windows.

Systems Over Emotion

Every trade logged. Performance is a system. Emotions are liabilities. Discipline is the only edge that lasts.

Risk Obsession

Capital preservation first. Position sizing, R:R discipline, and drawdown management. Profits are secondary.

What I Track

Every trade. Every signal missed. Every market condition. This isn't a hobby — it's a business built on data.

Win Rate: Steady improvement over time. Not the focus — consistency is.

R:R Ratio: Target 1:2 minimum. Risk $1 to make $2.

Drawdown: Managed monthly. Capital preservation is rule #1.

Session Breakdown: London (highest volatility), NY (trend confirmation), Asia (pattern setting).

About Me

Trader · Strategist · Systems Thinker

I'm Parth — a full-time intraday trader with 4+ years in forex, gold, and crypto markets. My edge isn't in complex algorithms or AI predictions. It's in understanding price action, respecting risk, and executing with precision.

Everything I trade, I build systems around. Every loss, I analyze ruthlessly. Every win, I reverse-engineer.

Core Philosophy

🎯 Price Action First

Charts don't lie. News, sentiment, macro — they all move price. I read what the market is actually doing, not what I think it should do.

⏰ Session Precision

Not all hours are equal. London open, NY open — these have personality. I trade when conditions match my edge.

🛡️ Risk First, Profit Second

Position sizing isn't boring. It's the only thing that actually compounds wealth. Every trade starts with: "How much am I willing to lose?"

⚙️ Systems > Feelings

Emotions are expensive. I build rules, I follow them, I backtest them. The moment I improvise, I lose money.

The Journey

2020 — Starting Out

Learned forex on demo, blew up real account in 3 weeks. Realized I knew nothing. Started reading, studying, failing.

2021 — Finding the Edge

Stopped chasing indicators. Went back to basics — support, resistance, structure. Started paper trading seriously. First consistent month.

2022 — Building Systems

Started tracking trades obsessively. Built my own Excel journal. Realized psychology was 80% of the game. Focused on execution, not prediction.

2023–2024 — Going Full-Time

Scaled account from $5K to consistent monthly income. Added gold and crypto. Built frameworks for different market conditions. Now trade live daily.

2025 — Sharing the Craft

Building this site. Not to teach, but to document. Not to sell a course, but to show what real trading looks like — wins, losses, the whole picture.

What This Isn't

❌ Not a guru — I don't have a "system" to sell.

❌ Not predicting pips — Nobody can predict the next 100 pips. That's fantasy.

❌ Not saying it's easy — Trading is hard. Consistent trading is harder.

What This Is

✓ Real trades — Real P&L, real psychology, no sugar-coating.

✓ A journal — What works, what doesn't, lessons from both.

✓ For engineers — If you think systematically, you'll resonate here.

✓ Proof of concept — Consistent trading is possible. Not easy, but possible.
